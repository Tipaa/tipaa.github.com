Cycler - A bookmarklet for cycling between pages

Drag the Cycler link to the bookmarklets bar to add it to your browser

This bookmarklet is a portable version of the Cycler page up at LazyWebTools. To make it easier to launch from anywhere, I thought I'd make it so that you'd only click one button to get full functionality.

It should work on all browsers with bookmarklet support. Thanks LazyWebTools (Cycler) and Firebug (Bookmarklet technique).
